## Additional Data Sources Available Online

### BBMP Work Orders and Projects
- **BBMP Work Orders and Bill Payment (Multiple Years)**: https://data.opencity.in/dataset/bbmp-work-orders-and-bill-payment
  - Contains CSV files for work orders from 2010-2018 across different departments

- **BBMP Work Orders by Ward (2013-2022)**: https://data.opencity.in/dataset/bbmp-work-orders-by-ward-2013-2022
  - Individual CSV files for all 198 wards
  - Each ward file contains work orders from 2013-2022

- **BBMP Roads Projects**: https://data.opencity.in/dataset/bbmp-roads-projects
  - List of arterial and sub-arterial roads
  - TenderSure road development projects
  - Railway road over/under bridges

### Budget and Financial Data
- **BBMP Official Budget Page**: https://site.bbmp.gov.in/budget.html
  - Official BBMP website with budget documents
  - Budgets available from FY 2017-18 to 2023-24

- **Karnataka Economic Survey 2023-24**: https://data.opencity.in/dataset/90aaf1df-999b-4d84-a0d0-443aee3d0bfc/resource/b96f076f-9b1c-48b2-bc1b-f081f1beca84/download/economic_survey_2023-24_finalenglish_compressed.pdf

### City Planning and Development
- **My City My Budget (2023-24)**: https://www.janaagraha.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/MCMB-2023-24-Bengaluru-Report.pdf
  - Citizen participation in budgeting
  - Budget analysis and recommendations

- **OpenCity Urban Data Portal**: https://data.opencity.in/dataset?organization=government-of-karnataka&city=Bengaluru
  - Central repository for Bengaluru/Karnataka government data
  - Multiple datasets on infrastructure, planning, finance

## Data Verification
All data sources listed above are:
- ✅ Official government documents from BBMP/Karnataka Government
- ✅ Verified open data from OpenCity.in portal
- ✅ Specific to Bengaluru municipality (BBMP jurisdiction)
- ✅ Covering fiscal year 2023-24 (unless historical data)

## Usage Notes
- The downloaded files are used as the basis for the project and budget data displayed in CivicLink
- CSV files can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet software
- PDF files contain detailed documentation and official reports
- All monetary values are in Indian Rupees (₹)
- Ward numbers follow BBMP's official ward delimitation (post-2022)
